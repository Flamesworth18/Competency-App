USM Faculty Competency Assessment
Instructions

On this Competency Assessment Booklet, you will find the Competency Standards of the Faculty Members of the University of Southern Mindanao. As a faculty member, you will be assessed on the six (6) competency clusters: Core, Leadership, Instruction, Research, Extension and Prodcution. You are to assess your level of competency according to BIAS assessment: Basic, Intermediate, Advanced, and Superior. Read carefully the behavioral indicators described in the BIAS matrix. Assess yourself which level your current competencies are. On the self-assessment page, please indicate your competency. After ascertaining your competency level, please describe an event where you have demonstrated your competencies (2016 - 2020 period). Please indicate also when the event happened. We suggest you follow the STAR guideline in responding to the essay on evidence: Describe a SITUATION where you needed to demonstrate the competency. In what capacity or what TASKS you performed it? What were your ACTIONS? What were the RESULTS? This form should be printed and sent to HRMDO.
